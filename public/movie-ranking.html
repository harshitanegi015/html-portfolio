The Best Movies According to Harshita

My top three movies of all time

The Hunger Games

I love how Katniss stands up for what's right and how the story blends survival, rebellion, and emotion in a gripping way.

Harry Potter and The Half Blood Prince

I'm drawn to the darker tone, the emotional depth, and how it reveals the secrets behind Voldemort's rise.

The Maze Runner

I enjoy the suspense, fast-paced action, and the mystery of the maze that keeps you guessing till the end.
